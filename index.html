Methodology. AUM, cost, and % G/L taken from the system-generated Portfolio Appraisal (account & group level, as of 30 Jun 2026). Allocation % = sleeve total MV ÷ fund total MV (₹). % G/L is the statement's reported Gain/Loss ÷ Cost; reported gain excludes accrued income (₹) that is included in Market Value, so it is not simply MV − Cost. Market-cap split covers the equity portion only (cash excluded) and is mapped from Mcap classification.xlsx. Overlap is computed on the 5 equity strategies; the Cash Portfolio is excluded from market-cap and overlap views.
